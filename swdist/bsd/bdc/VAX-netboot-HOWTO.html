The VAX Network Booting HOWTO

Brian Chase, bdc@world.std.com

v0.5, 31 Jan 1998

This is a guide to network booting DEC VAXstation and MicroVAX systems with NetBSD/vax from a Unix based bootserver. The information in this guide does not apply in a general way to bootserving. It is specifically geared towards MOP initiated bootserving as is common to DEC's line of VAX computers. The latest version of this document can be obtained from: http://world.std.com/~bdc/projects/vaxen/ Comments, questions, and corrections are welcome and expected.
