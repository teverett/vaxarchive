vaxine.bitcon.no, a MicroVAX II

Click on picture to see even larger version (95K)

Vaxine spent the first ten years of her life running the accounting system for a company here in Bergen, Norway. Back then she was running VAX/VMS and she was part of a VAXcluster.

Today she is of course running NetBSD/vax and very happy that she hasn't been retired (or worse, she doesn't even want to think about that!)

Other stuff she is running includes the Apache Web server, Perl5, the bash shell, the xntpd network time sync daemon and more.

She would like some more memory as she has only got 9 MB. On the other hand she has got plenty of disk (1.2 GB Seagate Wren VII SCSI on an MSCP-SCSI controller), serial lines (2 x DHV11) and of course a TK50 tape drive.

You can read more about the technical specifications of the MicroVAX II here.
